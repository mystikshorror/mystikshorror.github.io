Ghost Asked for a Selfie — UiTM Students Encounter Cik Pon

By Iskandar Aizat · Date 10 Jun 2026 · Category Paranormal
🌐 Baca dalam Bahasa Malaysia BM

SHAH ALAM — While most people would bolt in the opposite direction upon encountering a mysterious figure in the dead of night, a group of Universiti Teknologi MARA (UiTM) students apparently did the very opposite — they allegedly asked “Cik Pon” for a selfie after running into her near the college residence area.

The incident, which went viral on social media, reportedly occurred around 12.30 in the morning, when several students were walking back to their college after a group assignment discussion.

Witnesses

According to a student who wishes to be known only as Amir, they initially assumed the silhouette of a white-clad, long-haired woman standing near a large tree was another student making social media content.

“We spotted her from a distance. At first we thought a senior was filming a TikTok. When we got closer, the atmosphere turned strange because she just stood there, completely still.” — Amir

What made it even more remarkable was the reaction of some of the other students, who reportedly showed no sign of fear whatsoever. Instead, they joked around — some even asking permission to take a photo with the mysterious figure.

“One of my friends actually said, ‘Kak, can we get a selfie? We want something to remember our final semester by’,” — Amir

According to witnesses, the figure then vanished suddenly after a few seconds, sending the atmosphere into chaos. Though many were frightened afterwards, the video footage of the incident triggered a wave of humorous reactions online.

Netizen Reactions

In the comment sections on social media, many joked about the audacity of today's younger generation.

“Back in the day, you'd see a pontianak and pass out on the spot. These days, they ask for Instagram first.” — Social Media User

Another netizen quipped that the figure was likely so taken aback by the students' boldness that it chose to disappear.

The authenticity of the video, however, has yet to be confirmed. There are those who believe it may simply be a prank or content created purely for entertainment.

The university, when contacted, stated that no official report of any paranormal incident had been received. Students were nonetheless advised to always prioritise their safety and avoid being alone in quiet areas late at night.

Whether real or just another campus legend blown out of proportion, the tale of “Cik Pon” has become a hot topic among the university community. What's certain is this: if anyone truly encounters a mysterious figure in future, some will probably spend more time looking for the best camera angle than looking for a way out.
