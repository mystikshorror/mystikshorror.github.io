Ever been deep in one of our horror stories and suddenly your stomach growls like a ghost knocking at the door?

If so, come closer — because Risk has some news about a brand new menu at our good friends over at Burger Tophat. Available in Puncak Alam, Gerik, Pasir Gudang, and Gombak. (Apologies to our friends in Borneo.)

Spooky Menu and Limited Edition Merchandise

Here's how it happened: our collaboration partner — through Burger Tophat LLC's Chief Corporate Relations Officer, Encik Muhammad Nirman Atif — recently ran into me (your Chief Editor) at a library. (Yes, we're nerds.) It turned out that Abang Nirman here, despite his RM10,000 monthly salary (after all mandatory and non-mandatory deductions), is a die-hard fan of ours.

As luck would have it, we bumped into Burger Tophat LLC's Chief Web Engineer — Encik Muhammad Irfan, also known as Ipan Bintang — at a stone table outside a cafe called Bayu Aroma. We hung around, and then, like a bolt of inspiration from Man Bai himself, it hit us: “What if we did a collab?”

“Yesterday is history, Tomorrow is mystery, Today is Chews-day, let us burger-mbira.” — Ipan Bintang, Chief Web Engineer Burger Tophat LLC, 11 Jun 2026 / Encik Irfan forgot that 11 June 2026 is a Thursday, not a Tuesday

Spooky Menu at Burger Top Hat, 6 July to 7 August

From that outing — where our intention was to “formalise” the collaboration — we held another meeting, where a great deal more was discussed. The menu is already set; all that's left is for you to come try it at the event.

We received from our friends at Burger Tophat the specific menu for this collaboration only. Them being (IN VERY DAPPER ENGLISH) the Crimson Beef Burger, the Void Chicken Burger, the Midnight Sundae, and the Obsidian Fish.
