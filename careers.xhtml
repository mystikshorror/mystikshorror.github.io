■ Open Recruitment For Hobbyist Writers

Tulis Bersama Kami. | Write With Us.

Anda ada cerita? Anda ada keberanian? Anda sanggup pergi ke tempat orang lain tak nak pergi. Kalau anda berdiri di atas kerusi dan teriak "YA!" — anda tidak sayang nyawa anda! Sertai kami, kami mempunyai kadar kecederaan di tempat kerja paling tinggi di dalam industri! (Daripada Sindrom Karpal Tunel).

Kami sentiasa mencari mereka yang ingin tahu, mereka yang berani dan mengambil serius perkara luar nadar. — tanpa menjadi terlampau serius dalam perwatakan.

Do you have a story? Are you brave? Are you willing to go to places that no one in their right mind wants to go to? If you answered with "YES" — you have no survival instinct! Join us, we have the highest workplace injury rates in the industry! (From Carpal Tunnel Syndrome).

We are always on the lookout for curious, fearless contributors who take the unexplained seriously — without taking themselves too seriously.

Rasa Ingin Tahu Yang Tak Boleh Dipadamkan | Undying Curiosity

Anda dengar nenek sebelah rumah kata sesuatu itu sueh, dan anda tanya nenek itu. Anda layak.

You hear the grandma next door say stuff about bad luck, and you immediately ask them about it. You're qualified.

Tidak Buta Huruf | Can Read and Write

Tak perlu ijazah. Perlukan keupayaan untuk ceritakan sesuatu dengan jelas, jujur, dan tanpa lebih-lebih drama.

No Bachelor's needed. You only need the ability to tell a story with clarity, honesty and without melodrama

⊝
Skeptikal Tapi Terbuka | Open-minded Sceptic

Mystics bukan tim buru hantu, bukan tim paranormal yang asyik teriak, "Jelmakan dirikau". Kami wartawan. Anda perlu mencari logik di sebalik batu — walaupun jawapannya agak kelainan.

Mystics is not a paranormal team like Ed and Lorraine, or the Nerds from Insidious, we're journalists. You should find the logic behind each event — even if it is a bit out of the norm.
